Welcome to Blackjack!

Rules:

There are three buttons: restart, hit, and stand. If you think your card value is too small, you can hit. If you think your card value is too large, you can stand. If you lose or simply want to try next round, you can restart.

You win automatically if you get a blackjack (ace plus ten) in the beginning. Note ace could be treated either as 1 or 11. Also note if you choose to stand, the dealer (computer) will keep hitting until its card values sum up to more than 17.
